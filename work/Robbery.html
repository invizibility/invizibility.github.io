Armed Robbery
Project Type:
Pencil + Charcoal Drawing
Materials Used:
  • Graphite Pencil
  • Medium Weight Drawing Paper
  • Charcoal
Dimensions:
18"x24"
Year:
2010
Project Description:

I completed this project in an intermediate level drawing course at UW-Madison. We were requested to create a charcoal and pencil drawing that explored "vision" and related optical metaphors. The subject of my drawing is the surveillance footage of an armed robbery taking place.
