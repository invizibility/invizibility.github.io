Project Description:

This project was one of my main efforts during the Summer 2016 Data Visualization Internship at the NASA Jet Propulsion Lab + Caltech. In collaboration with fellow intern Peter Polack, I worked with the Guttman Lab for lncRNA biology to devise a workflow for exploring complex datasets describing the 3 dimensional conformation of genomic data inside cells, so as to identify potentially interesting patterns. The recent increased availability of such structural datasets has many genomicists interested in studying relationships between genomic structure and function, which necessitates the creation of new visualization tools not only for representation but for hypothesis generation.

I am currently extending this work as part of my thesis research at Carnegie Mellon.
