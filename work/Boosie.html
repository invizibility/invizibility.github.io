  • Canvas
Dimensions:
18"x24"
Year:
2011
Project Description:

This was another project assigned in Derrick Buisch's color theory class I took at UW-Madison. In this painting, we were asked to explore the range of grayscale-like values that exist between 3 sets of complementary colors (red/green, blue/orange, yellow/purple). Additionally, for each of the 3 complementary pairs we were required to include a section in the painting where the hues "vibrate" against one another. The subject of my painting was a still shot of the Lil Boosie music video for his song "We Outchea".
