Project Description:

I did this project as an assignment in Derrick Buisch's color theory class at UW Madison. We were asked to create a painting utilizing a range of 9 grays from black to white, while also integrating the same range of values for a chosen hue. The subject of my painting is a still shot of Aaliyah from the movie "Queen of the Damned".
